  • utm-lat-lng

    Sometimes SAR alerts are sent with a UTM coordinate describing where to link up or where to extract the patient. I could not find a practical app to convert UTM to latitude and longitude, so I wrote this Java package. The conversion is based on the Thompson-Lee series, a variant of Gauss-Krüger.

    Features include:

    • Converting latitude and longitude to UTM coordinate
    • Converting UTM coordinate to latitude and longitude
    • Parsing UTM coordinate with latitude band
    • Parsing UTM coordinate with hemisphere
